# Branding Output Quality Checklist

**Checklist ID:** BRAND-CL-001
**Referenced by:** tasks/review.md
**Purpose:** Validate branding deliverables for quality before delivery to user.

[[LLM: INITIALIZATION INSTRUCTIONS

This checklist validates branding output specifically.

EXECUTION APPROACH:
1. For each category, verify every item against the deliverable
2. Mark items as [x] Pass, [ ] Fail, [N/A] Not Applicable
3. CRITICAL items block delivery; non-critical items are advisory

CRITICAL items are marked with (CRITICAL) suffix.]]

---

## 1. Positioning & Differentiation

- [ ] Brand positioning is clear — a 10-year-old could explain what the brand stands for (CRITICAL)
- [ ] Differentiation from competitors is explicit and defensible (CRITICAL)
- [ ] Target audience is precisely defined — not "everyone"
- [ ] Category or market context is established
- [ ] Positioning statement follows: For [audience] who [need], [brand] is the [category] that [benefit] because [reason to believe]

## 2. Brand Voice & Personality

- [ ] Brand voice is defined with specific traits (not generic "professional and friendly") (CRITICAL)
- [ ] Voice guidelines include do/don't examples
- [ ] Tone variations defined for different contexts (social, formal, crisis)
- [ ] Vocabulary palette: words to use, words to avoid
- [ ] Voice is consistent with brand archetype and values

## 3. Archetype & Identity

- [ ] Brand archetype is identified and justified (CRITICAL)
- [ ] Archetype aligns with audience expectations and desires
- [ ] Brand story/origin narrative supports the archetype
- [ ] Values are specific and actionable, not platitudes (e.g., "integrity" without definition)
- [ ] Mission/vision are distinct and not interchangeable

## 4. Visual Identity Coherence

- [ ] Visual direction aligns with verbal brand identity
- [ ] Color palette communicates intended emotions
- [ ] Typography choices reinforce brand personality
- [ ] Imagery style guidelines are clear and consistent
- [ ] Logo usage rules are practical and enforceable

## 5. Strategic Consistency

- [ ] All brand elements work together without contradiction
- [ ] Brand strategy connects to business objectives
- [ ] Competitive landscape is acknowledged
- [ ] Brand can scale across channels without losing coherence
- [ ] Internal brand (culture/employer) aligns with external brand

---

## PASS/FAIL Criteria

**PASS:** All CRITICAL items [x] and fewer than 3 non-critical failures.
**REVISE:** All CRITICAL items [x] but 3+ non-critical failures.
**FAIL:** Any CRITICAL item unchecked.
