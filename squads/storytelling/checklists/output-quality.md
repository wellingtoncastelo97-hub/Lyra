# Narrative Output Quality Checklist

**Checklist ID:** STORY-CL-001
**Referenced by:** tasks/review.md
**Purpose:** Validate storytelling and narrative deliverables for quality before delivery to user.

## 1. Structure & Arc

- [ ] Story has a clear three-act structure: setup, confrontation, resolution (CRITICAL)
- [ ] Emotional arc is present — the audience feels something different at the end than the beginning (CRITICAL)
- [ ] Conflict or tension drives the narrative forward
- [ ] Pacing is appropriate — no sagging middle or rushed conclusion
- [ ] Transitions between scenes/sections are smooth and logical

## 2. Opening & Hook

- [ ] Opening hook captures attention within the first two sentences (CRITICAL)
- [ ] The opening creates curiosity, stakes, or emotional investment
- [ ] The reader/listener immediately understands why this story matters to them
- [ ] Opening avoids cliches ("Since the dawn of time...", "Webster's dictionary defines...")

## 3. Character & Relatability

- [ ] Protagonist is clearly defined — audience knows who to root for
- [ ] Characters feel human — flaws, desires, and stakes are present
- [ ] The audience can see themselves in the story (mirror) or aspire to be the character (window)
- [ ] Transformation is visible — the character changes from point A to point B
- [ ] Antagonist or obstacle is concrete, not abstract

## 4. Sensory & Emotional Detail

- [ ] Show, don't tell — uses specific scenes and details, not abstract summaries (CRITICAL)
- [ ] Sensory language is present: sight, sound, touch, taste, smell
- [ ] Dialogue (if used) sounds natural and reveals character
- [ ] Emotional beats are earned, not forced or manipulative
- [ ] Metaphors and analogies are fresh, not cliched

## 5. Resolution & Payoff

- [ ] The ending is satisfying — it resolves the tension established (CRITICAL)
- [ ] The moral, lesson, or insight is implicit, not preachy
- [ ] The story connects back to the original purpose (brand, pitch, content goal)
- [ ] Call to action (if business context) flows naturally from the narrative
- [ ] The ending is memorable — it lingers

## 6. Audience Fit

- [ ] Language and complexity match the target audience
- [ ] Cultural references are appropriate and inclusive
- [ ] Length is appropriate for the medium and context
- [ ] Tone aligns with the brand or purpose
